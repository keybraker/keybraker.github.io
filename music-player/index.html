Music Player

You can enter

Video names, Youtube URLs, Youtube playlist URLs or portal_video_queue.json

Type it, Play it!

You don't need to learn any command, simply type the video you wish to listen to and Portal will do the rest for you !

Easy and intuitive

You don't need to type anything to just play and pause.
Simply tap the pause button, and the video is paused.
When you are ready to continue listening, just tap play !

Sometime you have to listen on repeat

Is a song stuck in your head, embrace it with pinned videos. Simply pin the video and Portal will replay it, once it is over.

Volume controls

You can control the volume of Portal to streamline the experience for all the users, with two simple clicks.

Add to queue and skip to it

When requesting a song and Portal already playing a video, it is added to the queue. Simple skip to the next song !

The DJ is in the house

DJ's are important, and that is why there is one ! Simply assign role p.dj to make him a DJ.

Playlist auto insert

Automatic playlist insertion. Simply enter the playlist link and start listening to your favorite music, without any breaks !

Finished listening

Are you done with Portal?
Just show Portal the door and he will clear the queue and leave.
